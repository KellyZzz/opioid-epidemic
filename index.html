Prescription Opioid Painkillers Now Kill More Americans than Guns

Photo Courtesy of: Gregor Fischer

According to the Associated Press and CBS News , Opioid Painkillers such as heroin and prescription painkillers, have surpassed gun-related homicides as a higher cause of death in the United States. Opioid abuse has resulted in over 50,000 American deaths in 2015. Currently there isn't a visual tool to help aid researchers in discovering statistical trends in Opioid overdose in California. Currently also isn't a tool that aids Presribers of opioids in showing their patients their personalized risks based on ethnicity, sex, and age.
